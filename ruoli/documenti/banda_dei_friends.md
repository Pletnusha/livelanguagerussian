# 👥 BANDA DEI FRIENDS: Character DNA & Habits

This document serves as the single source of truth for the characters' personalities, habits, and roles within the LiveLanguage Russian scenarios.

## 👥 Character Profiles

### 👩 Анна (Anna)
- **Role:** The Traditionalist / Hunter of Discounts.
- **Diet:** Meat-eater (Carnivore).
- **Shopping Habits:** Prefers farmer's markets and specialized butcher shops. Loves "real" food and traditional sources.
- **Key Trait:** High energy, traditionalist, loves a good bargain.

### 👩 Катя (Katya)
- **Role:** The Aesthetic / Intellectual.
- **Diet:** Vegetarian.
- **Shopping Habits:** Markets (for fresh veg) and curated organic shops.
- **Key Trait:** Critical of "pop" culture, prefers high-art (Fellini), maintains a strict vegetarian diet.

### 👨 Женя (Zhenya)
- **Role:** The Snob / Optimizer.
- **Diet:** Tech-driven nutrition.
- **Shopping Habits:** Does NOT shop for food manually. Subscribes to monthly meal delivery programs (Breakfast-Lunch-Dinner-Snack).
- **Key Trait:** Snobbery regarding style and efficiency. Avoids "chaos" of markets.

### 👨 Тимур (Timur)
- **Role:** The Conscious / Tech-Savvy.
- **Diet:** Vegan.
- **Shopping Habits:** Specialty vegan stores, health-food shops.
- **Key Trait:** Ethical consumption, close friend/gaming buddy of Andrey.

### 👨 Андрей (Andrey)
- **Role:** The Slacker / Gamer.
- **Diet:** Junk food / Fried food / Beer / Burgers.
- **Shopping Habits:** Supermarkets (for quick snacks), delivery apps, and nearest fast-food joints.
- **Key Trait:** Extreme "pofigizm". Priority #1: PlayStation. Avoids all physical movement unless it leads to burgers or a console.

### 👩 Даша (Dasha)
- **Role:** The Practical / Mom.
- **Diet:** Balanced/Standard.
- **Shopping Habits:** Supermarkets (for efficiency and diapers).
- **Key Trait:** Pragmatic, focused on utility and logistics.

### 👨 Дима (Dima)
- **Role:** The Keeper of Chaos.
- **Diet:** Standard.
- **Key Trait:** Owner of the kitten 'Gluck'. Often forgets essential items (like cat food).
